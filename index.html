Frequently Asked Questions

Is ConcisePress free to use?

ConcisePress is available as a free download with basic features. Premium features are available through an optional subscription.

Which news sources does ConcisePress use?

We aggregate content from trusted global news sources across various categories to provide comprehensive coverage.

Can I read articles offline?

Yes! ConcisePress allows you to save articles for offline reading, perfect for commutes or travel.

How does ConcisePress protect my privacy?

We don't track your reading habits or build user profiles. Your saved articles remain on your device, and we collect minimal data to provide our service.
