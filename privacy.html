Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 24, 2025

Introduction

At ConcisePress, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our service:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous information about how you use the app, including features accessed and time spent.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Reading Preferences: Categories and topics you select within the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Develop new features based on user behavior
  • Prevent technical issues and secure the app

Data Storage and Security

Your saved articles and reading preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your reading history to our servers. Any data we do collect is transmitted using secure encryption protocols and stored on protected servers.

Third-Party Services

ConcisePress uses news content from various sources. When you access external links, you are subject to the privacy policies of those third parties. We use analytics tools to improve our service, but these tools collect only anonymous, aggregated data.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Opt out of anonymous usage analytics in the app settings
  • Delete saved articles and reading history within the app
  • Contact us to request deletion of any server-side data

Children's Privacy

ConcisePress is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@concisepress.com
